Black Bean Brownies

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup of black beans
  2. 2 tbso cocoa powder
  3. 1/2 cup almond flower
  4. 1/4 tsp salt
  5. 1/2 cup of agave
  6. 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  7. 2 tsp vanilla extract
  8. 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. 2/3 cup of non dairy chocolate chips (my favorite brand is EnjoyLife)

Instructions

  1. preheat the overn to 350 F
  2. combine all ingredients in a food processor and blend very well. This may take a few minuets, the smoother the better.
  3. Pot into an 8x8 greased pan
  4. add extra chocolate chips to the top of the batter for decoration
  5. bake for 16 minuets!
  6. My faovirte way to eat these is after letting them sit in the freezer for 15-20 minetes after they have cooled, then keeping them in the fridge to eat later.

Gluten Free Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 cup of sugar
  2. 1 1/2 cups of almond flour
  3. 1 cup cocoa powder
  4. 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. 1 tsp salt
  6. 2/3 cup vegetable oil
  7. 4 eggs
  8. 1 1/3 cups water
  9. 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. preheat the overn to 350 F
  2. whisk together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. beat the oil, eggs and water into the bowl.
  4. once all the ingredients are mixed, beat the batter for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour batter into 2 9 inch circular pans.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes.

Chocolate Covered Strawberries

Ingredients

  1. 15-20 strawberries
  2. one bag of enjoyLife chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Melt enjoyLife chocolate in a sauce man on medium-low heat until smooth.
  2. Once the choclate is melted, dip the strawberries in the chocolate and place on wax paper to dry.
  3. Let the covered strawberries sit in the fridge for 1-3 hourss.
